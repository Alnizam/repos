Cookie Balls

Have you ever had a weird dream about playing football with cookies? Well, I had... Here's incredible cookie balls for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 pund vanilla-falvored candy coating, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine crushed cookies and cream cheese to form a stiff dough. Roll into balls and dip with a fork in melted candy coating. Let rest on waxed paper until set.
